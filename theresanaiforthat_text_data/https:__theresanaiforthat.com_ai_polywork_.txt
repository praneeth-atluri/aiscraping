Polywork is an AI-powered tool that allows users to create a personal website in minutes. The tool aims to help individuals showcase their work, grow their personal brand, or start a side hustle.  With Polywork, users can generate personalized content for their website using AI, including bios, headlines, and resumes. The tool offers a block library that enables users to add diverse content types to represent their individuality beyond their job title.  Users can also customize their website's layout, color schemes, and typography using the AI styler feature.In addition to these features, Polywork provides mobile app support, allowing users to edit and update their website content on the go.  The tool also offers contact controls, where users can set preferences for the type of opportunities they want to be contacted for. Users can track their website's performance through site analytics to optimize their content.  Polywork allows users to connect an existing custom domain or obtain a new domain for their website.Polywork is used by professionals in various industries to develop their personal brand, grow their side income, and differentiate themselves in the market.
