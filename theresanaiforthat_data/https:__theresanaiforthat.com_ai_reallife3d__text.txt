What is Real Life 3D?
Real Life 3D is an AI-powered tool that specializes in converting 2D images into 3D images for popular VR and 3D platforms like YouTube VR and Oculus.
 
How does Real Life 3D convert 2D images to 3D?
Real Life 3D uses a unique workflow that involves a recipe of processes leveraging the power of AI. This allows them to quickly and cost-effectively process a large number of frames, reducing the time and human resources required.
 
What platforms are compatible with Real Life 3D conversions?
Real Life 3D conversions are compatible with all popular VR and 3D platforms, including but not limited to YouTube VR and Oculus.
 
Can Real Life 3D process film and still images for 3D conversion?
Yes, Real Life 3D specializes in the conversion of both video and still imagery into 3D.
 
What makes Real Life 3D's workflow unique?
Real Life 3D's workflow is unique in its utilisation of a set of processes that harness the power of AI. This approach enables the processing of a large number of frames quickly and cost-effectively, making 3D conversion more accessible.
 
How cost-effective is Real Life 3D compared to other 3D conversion tools?
According to Real Life 3D, their AI-powered workflow reduces the time and human resources required for 3D conversion, making it a cost-effective solution for more than just feature films.
 
Is Real Life 3D suitable for creators and headset owners?
Yes, Real Life 3D is particularly suited for content creators and headset owners who wish to create immersive experiences.
 
What was the motivation for creating Real Life 3D?
The motivation behind creating Real Life 3D was a passion for storytelling through film and photography, and a desire to add an extra dimension to viewing these experiences in the form of immersive 3D.
 
What are the output formats provided by Real Life 3D?
Real Life 3D offers various output formats for 3D and VR platforms, including side-by-side, VR180, and anaglyph outputs.
 
What is the role of AI in Real Life 3D?
AI plays a critical role in Real Life 3D's operations; it powers their unique workflow and enables them to quickly and cost-effectively process a large number of frames for 3D conversion.
 
How quick is the Real Life 3D conversion process?

Yes, Real Life 3D showcases its capabilities on its website with a video that demonstrates its abilities, and there are also examples available on their YouTube Channel.
 
How can I deliver my content to 3D and VR platforms with Real Life 3D ?
With Real Life 3D, you can deliver your content to all 3D and VR platforms such as YouTube VR, Oculus, and others in formats like side-by-side, VR180, and anaglyph outputs.
 
Can Real Life 3D be used to create immersive experiences from life changing moments?
Yes, the creators of Real Life 3D express their interest in creating immersive experiences from life-changing moments using their tool.
 
What types of stories can be told with Real Life 3D?
Real Life 3D can be used to tell all kinds of stories including but not limited to travel, journalism, and historic stories. Essentially, it can turn any experience captured in 2D into a 3D experience.
