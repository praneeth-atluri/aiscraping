What is ImageCreator?
ImageCreator is a free text-to-image creator that operates as a Photoshop plugin. It's an art asset generator for Photoshop, allowing users to create attractive visuals. This tool enables users to generate images via TXT2IMG, IMG2IMG, and INPAINTING methods. With numerous control models, process settings, and stunning diffusion models, ImageCreator offers a user-friendly and straightforward method to create unique art directly from Photoshop.
 
What features does ImageCreator offer?
ImageCreator offers several remarkable features. Stable Diffusion, LoRA, ControlNet, and Generative Fill are among them. The tool allows users to create images using three different methods: TXT2IMG, IMG2IMG, and INPAINTING. It also provides prompt editing capabilities, enabling users to provide positive and negative prompts. These prompts can be edited using a personal notebook editor. ImageCreator also supports multiple control models and process settings for accurate results.
