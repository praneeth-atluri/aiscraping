Q&A
 
What is Foqus.live?
Foqus.live is a cloud-based video analytics service specifically designed for offline businesses. The tool utilizes real-time AI vision to provide visitor analytics, service performance analysis, heat maps, and even facial recognition features.
 
How does Foqus.live work?
Foqus.live works by integrating with the existing setup, which includes IP cameras. The system continuously collects and analyzes data from these cameras, providing immediate insights into your business operations. This data can then be used to make informed decisions, drive improvements, and achieve business objectives.
 
What are the technical requirements for using Foqus.live?
The technical requirements for using Foqus.live involve having the necessary equipment in place, including IP cameras, a stable internet connection, and a device that runs on either Windows, macOS or Linux.
 
What kind of equipment does Foqus.live require?
Foqus.live requires specific equipment, such as an IP-camera or multiple cameras, a stable internet connection, and a device with an operating system like Windows, macOS, or Linux.
 
Does Foqus.live provide visitor analytics?
Yes, Foqus.live provides visitor analytics. These include statistics and data related to the number and behavior of visitors, which can be used to optimize business operations.
 
What kinds of businesses can benefit from Foqus.live?
Foqus.live can benefit a wide range of offline businesses such as retail stores, factories, warehouses, offices, health and sports facilities, and hospitality businesses such as restaurants, cafes, and hotels.
 
What are the pricing plans for Foqus.live?
Foqus.live offers three pricing plans based on business needs: 'Per 1 Camera' plan priced at $39-$80, featured with visitor analytics, service performance analysis, heat map, and face recognition, 'Pro' plan priced at $249-$500 with features like people counter, service time counter, heat map, and face recognition, and an 'Enterprise' plan - the specifics and pricing of which can be learned via direct contact.
 
Are there any unique features in Foqus.live?
Foqus.live offers unique features like people counter, service time counter, heat maps, and face recognition. These features are tailored to help offline businesses optimize their operations and improve customer experience and service.
