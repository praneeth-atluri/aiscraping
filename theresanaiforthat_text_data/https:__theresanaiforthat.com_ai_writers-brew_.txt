Writer's Brew is an AI assistant designed to help users accelerate their reading and writing 10x faster and better. It works across all browsers, native apps, and electron apps, allowing users to access it from any platform.  It comes with 50+ presets, including 'Explain Like I'm 5' and 'Summarize & Takeaways'. Additionally, it has a 'Smart Reply to Email' feature that allows users to generate text directly in text editors or in a mini window.The Raycast and Writer's Brew AI integration allows users to do things such as fix grammar, improve fluency, translate text, explain concepts like they're 5, and summarize content.  It also includes a 'Build Your Own Presets' (BYOP) feature, which allows users to create and save their own prompts as presets. Writer's Brew is available in three different ways: a dropdown menu, a handy menubar, and a full app experience.  It is cost effective, 10x-100x cheaper than other services. It is currently available for macOS, with an iOS version in the works. The Early Believers Pro Plan is available for $29, with a Launch Pro Plan for $49-$89.  The low cost of Writer's Brew is due to the use of the user's own OpenAI key and the avoidance of managing servers beyond minimum needs.

Q&A
 
What is Writer's Brew?
Writer's Brew is an AI assistant designed to accelerate users' reading and writing speed and quality. It operates across all browsers, native apps, and electron apps. The software boasts a selection of over 50 presets, including options like 'Explain Like I'm 5' and 'Summarize & Takeaways'. It incorporates the 'Brew Magic Writer' tool and a 'Smart Reply to Email' feature. Writer's Brew is cost-effective and offers up to 100x savings compared to other services. It is presently supported on macOS with an iOS version being developed.
 
How does Writer's Brew improve my writing speed?
Writer's Brew accelerates the writing process by providing structure and facilitating ideation with over 50 available presets including those that provide summary and key takeaways of existing content. Using artificial intelligence, it corrects grammar, improves fluency, and offers translation among other features. Its tools are designed for use across all the platforms you might use, including browsers, native, and electron apps.
 
What platforms are compatible with Writer's Brew?
Writer's Brew is compatible with all browsers and works seamlessly with native apps and electron apps, including macOS and Raycast. There's also a version currently under development for iOS.
 
What are the features of the 'Brew Magic Writer' tool in Writer's Brew?
The 'Brew Magic Writer' in Writer's Brew is a tool that generates creative pieces of writing from user inputs and instructions. The writer can provide instructions and the AI will generate text accordingly. It is capable of responding to tasks such as providing a blog title or an entire blog article, summarizing content, translating content, and acting as a proofreader and critique for your writing.
 
What does the 'Smart Reply to Email' feature on Writer's Brew do?
The 'Smart Reply to Email' feature in Writer's Brew not only helps generate text but also creates a draft in your email clients. This assists in rapidly composing and responding to emails.
 
How does the Raycast and Writer's Brew AI integration work?
Raycast and Writer's Brew work together to allow users to fix grammar, boost fluency, translate text, explain concepts as if to a five-year-old, summarize content, and more. It provides access to Writer's Brew and all its features within Raycast, creating a consolidated and intuitive user experience.
 
How can I use the 'Build Your Own Presets' feature on Writer's Brew?
With the 'Build Your Own Presets' (BYOP) feature, Writer's Brew allows users to customize their writing assistance. This feature allows you to create and save your own prompts as presets, providing a bespoke writing experience tailored to your individual preferences and needs.
 
Can I access the Writer's Brew tool on multiple devices?
Writer's Brew is designed for versatile access across multiple devices. It is currently available for macOS and a version for iOS is under development. Users can access Writer's Brew via a dropdown menu, a convenient menubar, and as a fully-fledged app.
 
What are the cost options for Writer's Brew?
Writer's Brew offers two plans for users - the Early Believers Pro Plan for $29 and the Launch Pro Plan for between $49-$89. The affordability stems from the platform's use of the user's own OpenAI key and eschewing of lavish server management
 
How is Writer's Brew more cost effective compared to other services?
Writer's Brew pulls off a cost-effective solution by utilizing the user's own OpenAI key and cutting back on server management beyond the bare minimum needs, lowering costs by 10x to 100x compared to comparable services.
 
Do I need my own OpenAI key to use Writer's Brew?
Yes, you would need your own OpenAI key to use Writer's Brew. This is one of the ways that the software manages to remain tremendously cost-effective.
 
Is there an iOS version of Writer's Brew?
An iOS version of Writer's Brew is currently in the works.
 
What does the Pro Plan offer in Writer's Brew?
The Pro Plan in Writer's Brew offers over 50 presets and the option to build your own presets. It also includes smart features, like 'Continue writing', 'Brew magic writer', smart email reply drafts, and a tool to convert image (OCR) to text. These capabilities are accessible from a dropdown menu, a menubar, and the full app. The Pro Plan integrates with Raycast. An iOS version is coming soon.
 
How does Writer's Brew intend to manage servers beyond minimum needs?
Writer's Brew intends to manage servers beyond minimum needs by limiting the use of server resources and utilizing the user's own OpenAI key.
 
Can Writer's Brew translate text to different languages?
Yes, Writer's Brew can translate text to different languages. It works seamlessly with Raycast to provide translations in a number of languages including English, French, German, Spanish, and Dutch.
 
How does the 'Image (OCR) to text' feature work in Writer's Brew?
The 'Image (OCR) to text' feature of Writer's Brew uses an OCR engine (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text from images, enabling it to process and interpret text in images for a more versatile and expansive writing experience.
 
In what ways can I use the Writer's Brew app?
Users can use the Writer's Brew app in three different ways - through a dropdown menu, a handy menubar, or as a full app. This flexibility caters to various user styles and preferences, maximizing usefulness and comfort.
 
What is the purpose of presets in Writer's Brew?
The purpose of presets in Writer's Brew is to provide ready-made prompts and guidelines to aid in writing, so users don't have to start from scratch. These not only facilitate quicker and more efficient writing but also cater to a range of diverse writing needs.
 
Can I customize my own presets in Writer's Brew?
Yes, Writer's Brew does allow the customization of presets. With the 'Build Your Own Presets' (BYOP) feature, you can write and save your own prompts, providing increased flexibility and customization to enhance your writing experience.
 
Does Writer's Brew offer a free version?
Writer's Brew does not offer a free version. Its pricing plans begin at $29 for the Early Believers Pro Plan, which includes a comprehensive suite of features and tools designed to assist and streamline writing.
