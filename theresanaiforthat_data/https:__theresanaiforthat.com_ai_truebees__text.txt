Q&A
 
What is TrueBees?
TrueBees is an AI-based tool designed to detect deepfakes, specifically AI-generated portraits. It helps users quickly and easily verify the trustworthiness of images before they are shared on social media platforms in order to prevent disinformation and cybercrime.
 
How does TrueBees work?
TrueBees uses a synergy of digital media forensics and blockchain technology for its operation. Forensic analysis algorithms verify the trustworthiness of an image portraying a human face, while blockchain provides a secure data structure where analysed images can be stored. The forensic analysis algorithms have been extensively trained and continue to learn from new data, leading to high levels of accuracy in deepfake detection.
 
What types of images does TrueBees analyze?
TrueBees focuses on fully synthetic images portraying human faces. It does not detect images that have been partially altered, screenshots or frames excerpted from deepfake videos, nor non-photorealistic images.
 
What industries is TrueBees designed for?
TrueBees is designed for professionals in the media industry and law firms. This includes press and news agencies, media outlets, freelance journalists, lawyers, and legal advisors.
 
Is it possible to integrate TrueBees with my own APIs?
Yes, it is possible to integrate TrueBees with your own APIs. This provides an additional layer of convenience and flexibility for its users.
 
How accurate is TrueBees at detecting deepfakes?
TrueBees has been designed, trained, and tested to detect deepfakes with a high accuracy rate of 97.99% on a reference test dataset.
 
What algorithms does TrueBees use for forensic analysis?
Specifics about the algorithms used by TrueBees for forensic analysis are not mentioned explicitly, but they are referred to as advanced, specially developed, and extensively trained for the purpose of verifying the trustworthiness of images.
 
Does TrueBees use blockchain technology and if so, why?
Yes, TrueBees does use blockchain technology. It provides a secure data structure where analysed images can be stored, ensuring both the integrity and the historical tracing of the authentication process.
 
What makes TrueBees different from other similar AI tools?
TrueBees is different from other similar AI tools in that it is the only known system capable of detecting AI-generated portraits after they've been shared on social media platforms, overcoming the problems faced by existing detectors using an innovative approach based on fine-tuned deep networks.
 
Can TrueBees detect deepfakes after they've been shared on social media?
Yes, TrueBees can detect AI-generated portraits not just in the raw format but also after they have been shared on social media platforms. This ability sets it apart from other existing detectors.
 
Who is TrueBees ideal for?
TrueBees is ideal for professionals in the press and news agencies, media outlets, freelance journalists, attorneys and legal advisors who need to ensure the veracity of the images they use. It is particularly useful for those seeking to combat the spread of deepfakes and misinformation on social media.
 
How can TrueBees help fight against disinformation and cybercrime?
TrueBees helps fight against disinformation and cybercrime by enabling the fast and accurate detection of AI-generated portraits or deepfakes, thus reducing the risk of these images being mistakenly shared on social media.
 
Can TrueBees authenticate online images?
Yes, TrueBees authenticates online images. It uses a synergistic approach combining digital media forensics and blockchain technology to ensure the authenticity of images.
 
How can TrueBees guarantee the trustworthiness of images?
TrueBees guarantees the trustworthiness of images by strictly verifying the image content using advanced forensic analysis algorithms. Once authenticated, these images are securely stored using blockchain technology.
 
Can TrueBees help in verifying images for lawsuits?
Yes, TrueBees can help verify images for lawsuits. It provides evidence on the trustworthiness of pictures used in such scenarios.
 
Why is TrueBees considered a 'good AI'?
TrueBees is considered a 'good AI' because it utilizes its capabilities to quickly detect and prevent the spread of potentially harmful and misleading deepfake images with a high level of accuracy.
 
Does TrueBees detect AI-generated portraits only or does it detect alterations in real images as well?
Currently, TrueBees only detects fully synthetic images that portray human faces. It does not detect images that have been partially altered.
 
How to create an account on TrueBees?
To create an account on TrueBees, one must go through a process that involves filling in a simple sign-up form and adding preferred payment methods and billing details.
 
Does TrueBees offer an API for developers?
Yes, TrueBees does offer an API for developers. This allows for seamless integration of TrueBees' deepfake detection capabilities with existing systems and processes.
 
Does TrueBees detect non-photorealistic AI-generated images like art, graphics, and renderings?
At present, TrueBees does not detect non-photorealistic AI-generated images like art, graphics, and renderings. It is focused on fully synthetic images that portray human faces.
