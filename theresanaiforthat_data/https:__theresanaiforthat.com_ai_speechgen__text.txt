Q&A
 
What is SpeechGen?
SpeechGen is an AI-powered text-to-speech converter that generates realistic voiceovers online.
 
What does SpeechGen.io do?
SpeechGen.io allows users to convert written text into speech. It offers features such as a multi-voice editor for creating dialogue with AI voices, downloadable TTS, long text support, commercial use, SSML support, cost saving measures, and compatibility with various editing programs. It also provides a cloud system for saving and sharing audio files and links.
 
Which languages are supported by SpeechGen?
SpeechGen supports multiple languages and dialects such as English (US & GB), Italian, German, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Turkish, Kazakh, Russian, Ukrainian, Norwegian, Uzbek, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, Hindi, Greek, Philippine, Finnish, Danish, Czech, Polish, Swedish, Indonesian, Korean, Romanian, Vietnamese, Latvian, Lithuanian, Estonian, Malay, Hebrew, and many others.
 
How many voices does SpeechGen offer?
SpeechGen offers over 270 natural-sounding voices for users to select and customize from.
 
Is there a limit to the amount of text I can convert to audio with SpeechGen?
Yes, there is a limit for text conversion. SpeechGen supports long texts up to 2 million characters.
 
Can I download the voiceovers created by SpeechGen?
Yes, users can download voiceovers produced by SpeechGen in audio formats such as mp3 and wav.
 
What formats can SpeechGen generate for downloaded audio?
SpeechGen can generate downloadable audio in mp3, wav and ogg formats.
 
What is SpeechGen's multi-voice editor?
SpeechGen's multi-voice editor is a functionality that allows users to create dialogues using multiple AI voices in one text.
 
Can I use SpeechGen for commercial purposes?
Yes, audio generated by SpeechGen can be used for commercial purposes, including but not limited to YouTube, Tik Tok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitch, Twitter, Podcasts, Video Ads, Advertising, E-books, Presentations, and others.
 
Does SpeechGen support SSML?
Yes, SpeechGen does support Speech Synthesis Markup Language (SSML), allowing for greater control and flexibility over how the text is converted into speech.
 
Can I modify the pitch and speed of the voices in SpeechGen?
Yes, in SpeechGen, users can modify the pitch and speed of the voices to fit their specific requirements.
 
Can I share my SpeechGen audio files or links with others?
Yes, users can share the links to their SpeechGen audio files with others.
 
How do I save my work on SpeechGen?
Users can save their work on SpeechGen automatically in their profile on SpeechGen's cloud server.
 
What types of voice settings can be customized through SpeechGen?
With SpeechGen, users can customize voice settings such as Speed, Pitch, Stress, Pronunciation, Intonation, Emphasis, and Pauses.
 
How does the pricing work on SpeechGen?
SpeechGen operates on a limit basis, where users can pay to increase their limit for speech generation in characters.
 
Can I use SpeechGen with other editing programs?
Yes, SpeechGen works with any video creation software including Adobe Premier, After Effects, Audition, DaVinci Resolve, Apple Motion, Camtasia, iMovie, Audacity, and more.
 
What is the quality of the voices generated by SpeechGen?
The voices generated by SpeechGen are of crystal-clear quality, mimicking human voices, and include male, female, children's, and elderly voices.
 
Are the voices generated by SpeechGen realistic?
Yes, the voices produced by SpeechGen are designed to be realistic and natural-sounding, closely resembling human voices.
 
Does SpeechGen have customer support for questions and issues?
Yes, SpeechGen provides powerful support for any questions users might have about text-to-speech conversion.
 
Can I create dialogues with multiple voices in SpeechGen?
Yes, through the multi-voice editor, users can create dialogues using multiple voices in one text with SpeechGen.
