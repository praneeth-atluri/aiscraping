Q&A
 
What is Zolak?
Zolak is a 3D product visualization software designed specifically for furniture retailers.
 
What features does Zolak offer for furniture retailers?
Zolak offers furniture retailers features such as the ability to visualize and personalize content about their products, an interactive and personalized product visualization tool called Zolak Vision, an interactive showroom called Zolak ShowRoom, ready-to-use components that integrate seamlessly into online stores, and 3D modeling services to assist retailers in showcasing their products effectively.
 
How does Zolak use AI and computer vision technologies to enhance online shopping experience?
Zolak uses AI and computer vision technologies to provide lifelike visualization of furniture. Its system allows retailers to personalize content about their products, enabling customers to view furniture pieces in various interior settings and experiment with replacing existing furniture in their own homes with new options.
 
How does Zolak help in boosting sales for furniture retailers?
Zolak helps in boosting sales by providing a virtual furniture showcase powered by AI and computer vision technology. This interactive and lifelike representation of products increases customer confidence, which subsequently leads to higher sales.
 
In what ways does Zolak help reduce return rates?
To help reduce return rates, Zolak allows customers to view furniture pieces in their own homes, experiment with putting different pieces together and visualize them from multiple angles. This gives customers a clear understanding of how a piece of furniture will look in their space, leading to more informed purchasing decisions and fewer returns.
 
How does Zolak deliver personalized digital experiences for customers?
Zolak delivers personalized digital experiences by offering customizable products that can be viewed from multiple angles and in various interior styles. The software also incorporates AI and computer vision technologies to provide a personalized virtual shopping experience.
 
What sets Zolak apart from standard augmented reality tools?
Unlike standard augmented reality tools, Zolak offers a more accurate and realistic visualization by considering factors like scaling, perspective, lighting, and shadows. It delivers these features without requiring multiple photo shoots and offers an intuitive interface that makes it accessible for non-designers as well.
 
Can Zolak produce photorealistic visuals?
Yes, Zolak produces photorealistic visuals. Through its advanced technology, it creates impressive photorealistic product visualizations, making them stand out to audiences.
 
How is Zolak better than traditional 3D design software?
Zolak is better than traditional 3D design software because of its user-friendly interface that allows non-designers to create stunning interiors with ease. It is also able to deliver photorealistic visuals that give a lifelike representation of furniture which classic 3D planners might not achieve.
 
How does Zolak surpass photo catalogues?
Zolak surpasses photo catalogues by providing interactivity and variability static images can't offer. It's ideal for brands with diverse product lines as it removes the need for customers to sift through countless static images.
 
What components are readily available in Zolak?
Zolak comes with readily-available components designed to integrate seamlessly into online stores which include personalized customization, 3D models for a superior customer experience, and varied settings and styles.
 
What support does Zolak offer its users?
Zolak offers ongoing support to its users to ensure smooth implementation and maintenance of the software.
 
Does Zolak provide 3D modeling services?
Yes, Zolak offers 3D modeling services to its clients. This aids in creating high-quality 3D models for a superior customer experience.
 
Can Zolak be customized according to a retailer's needs?
Zolak allows for on-demand customization, ready to develop tailor-made solutions to suit individual business requirements and objectives.
 
Can Zolak integrate into online stores?
Yes, Zolak's components are designed to quickly integrate into online stores for easy use.
 
How does Zolak help customers visualize products in their own space?
Zolak gives customers the ability to view furniture pieces in their own home environment. By replacing pieces in customer's photos with their furniture renderings based on perspective, scale and lighting, customers can visualize products in their own space increasing confidence in purchase decisions.
 
What is the benefit of Zolak ShowRoom for e-commerce store owners?
Zolak ShowRoom provides a virtual interactive showroom where e-commerce store owners can present their products in various interior settings. This solution gives customers a better idea of how the physical product would look in their space, bolstering their confidence in making purchases.
 
What is Zolak Vision and how does it aid online shoppers?
Zolak Vision is a personalized product visualization tool that allows online shoppers to view how furniture pieces would look in their own space by replacing existing furniture in the photo with a new one based on perspective, scale, and lighting.
 
How does Zolak help bridge the gap between offline and online shopping experiences?
Zolak bridges the gap between offline and online shopping experiences by providing a realistic preview of what the physical product will look like in a customer's space. It allows customers to view it from multiple angles in various interior styles, providing a shopping experience that closely resembles in-store shopping.
 
Why should a company consider using Zolak for its furniture retail?
Zolak offers numerous benefits for a furniture retail company. These include providing a realistic and interactive shopping experience, boosting customer confidence, reducing return rates, enabling content personalization, accelerating content creation and most importantly driving sales.
